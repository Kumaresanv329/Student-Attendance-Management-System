# Download Official Bharathiar University Logo

## 🎯 Official Logo Download Source

The official Bharathiar University logo is available for download from their official website:

**Direct Link:** https://b-u.ac.in/273/university-logo

## 📋 Steps to Download Official Logo:

1. **Visit the Official Website:**
   - Go to: https://b-u.ac.in/273/university-logo
   - Or visit: https://b-u.ac.in and navigate to "University Logo" section

2. **Download the Logo:**
   - The website provides logo files in various formats (PNG, JPG, SVG)
   - Choose PNG format for best quality (recommended)
   - Download the logo file

3. **Save to Project:**
   - Rename the downloaded file to: `logo.png`
   - Place it in: `frontend/public/logo.png`
   - The SVG placeholder will be automatically replaced

4. **Verify:**
   - Refresh your website
   - The official logo should now appear on login page and dashboard

## 📝 Logo Description:

The official logo features:
- **Orange gear-shaped outer ring** with 16 teeth
- **Central portrait** of Subramania Bharathi (Tamil poet)
- **Golden fish** (left) - Symbol of Pandyas kingdom
- **Golden tiger** (right) - Symbol of Cholas kingdom
- **Stack of five books** (red, green, blue) - Symbol of knowledge
- **Golden lamp (diya)** - Symbol of wisdom and enlightenment
- **Tamil text:** "பாரதியார் பல்கலைக்கழகம், கோயம்புத்தூர்"
- **English text:** "BHARATHIAR UNIVERSITY, Coimbatore"
- **Blue banner:** "EDUCATE TO ELEVATE" motto
